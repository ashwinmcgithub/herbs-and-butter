Our Story

A Dream Served Fresh,
Every Single Day

Herbs & Butter was born from a simple but passionate dream — to bring the rich, comforting flavours of authentic continental cuisine to the warm-hearted community of Thodupuzha.

What started as a quiet little spot has grown into one of Idukki's most beloved dining destinations. We believe that great food is more than sustenance — it's an experience, a memory, a reason to gather. Every pasta is rolled by hand. Every sauce is crafted from scratch. Every pizza stone-baked to perfection.

Our space is intentionally calm — a refuge for families, couples, solo readers, and remote workers alike. Come for the food, stay for the warmth.
